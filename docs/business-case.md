# Business Case

## Executive Summary

The organization managed multiple technology services and cybersecurity platforms, including Sophos Advanced Protection, Sophos Encryption, Sophos Email Security, COVE Backup, RMM services, professional services, and leased technology assets.

Monthly invoice validation and cost allocation activities were performed manually using multiple spreadsheets, requiring significant analyst effort and introducing operational and financial risks.

To improve efficiency, accuracy, and governance, a VBA-based automation solution was developed to automate cost allocation, consolidate information, and generate pre-invoice reports.

The solution reduced processing time from approximately 60 minutes to 10 minutes per billing cycle, representing an 83% improvement in operational efficiency.

---

## Business Challenge

The existing process required analysts to manually review supplier invoices and allocate technology-related costs to organizational cost centers.

The workflow involved:

1. Receiving supplier invoices.
2. Reviewing technology inventories.
3. Validating active licenses and assigned assets.
4. Identifying responsible users.
5. Matching users with cost centers.
6. Performing manual calculations.
7. Generating pre-invoice reports for validation.

The process relied heavily on spreadsheets and manual intervention.

---

## Key Issues Identified

### Operational Inefficiency

The process required approximately one hour of manual work during each billing cycle.

### Human Error Risk

Manual calculations and data entry increased the probability of allocation errors.

### Cost Allocation Inconsistencies

Technology expenses could be assigned incorrectly due to inventory discrepancies or data quality issues.

### Limited Traceability

The process lacked a standardized mechanism for tracking allocation decisions and validating results.

### Financial Risk

Incorrect allocations could lead to billing discrepancies and inaccurate cost distribution across business units.

---

## Proposed Solution

Develop an automated cost allocation framework using Microsoft Excel and VBA.

The solution would:

* Import technology inventories.
* Import user and cost center information.
* Standardize data.
* Match assets to responsible users.
* Calculate proportional allocations automatically.
* Consolidate information from multiple technology services.
* Generate pre-invoice reports.
* Produce executive summaries by cost center.
