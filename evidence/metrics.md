# Metrics and Business Impact

## Overview

This document summarizes the measurable operational improvements achieved through the implementation of the Technology Cost Allocation Automation solution.

The primary objective was to reduce manual effort, improve allocation accuracy, and strengthen governance over technology-related expenditures.

| Processing Time per Billing Cycle  | 60 Minutes        | 10 Minutes       | 83% Reduction         |
| Manual Allocation Activities       | High              | Minimal          | Significant Reduction |
| Spreadsheet Manipulation           | Extensive         | Automated        | Significant Reduction |
| Allocation Consistency             | Medium            | High             | Improved              |
| Traceability                       | Limited           | Improved         | Enhanced              |

# Time Savings Analysis

## Previous State

The original process required approximately:

* Invoice review.
* Inventory validation.
* Cost center identification.
* Manual calculations.
* Prefactura preparation.

Average effort:

**60 minutes per billing cycle**

---

## Automated State

After implementation:

* Data imports became standardized.
* Cost allocations became automated.
* Consolidation became automated.
* Prefactura generation became automated.

Average effort:

**10 minutes per billing cycle**
